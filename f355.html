F355 Berlinetta

Introduced in 1994, the F355 was designed to be an absolute driver's car. It bridged the gap between vintage mechanical sensations and modern aerodynamic performance.

The Legendary 5-Valve Engine

The heart of the car is its 3.5-liter V8 engine layout, featuring a unique 5-valves-per-cylinder cylinder head design. It yields 375 horsepower and sings with an unforgettable high-pitched exhaust note that screams all the way to its 8,500 RPM redline.

Performance Specs

With its low weight and slick aerodynamics, the F355 hits 0 to 100 km/h in just 4.7 seconds and pushes onward to a top velocity of 295 km/h. It was also the very first production road car to feature an F1-style paddle-shift transmission system.

F355 Highlights

  1. 3.5L Naturally Aspirated V8
  2. Innovative 5-Valve Design
  3. 6-Speed Manual / F1 Paddle Options
